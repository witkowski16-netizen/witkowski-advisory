About Evan Witkowski

Finance expertise meets engineering precision.

I bring a rare combination of deep institutional investment knowledge and hands-on technical skills in automation and AI. Where most consultants offer one or the other, I deliver both — which means I understand exactly where your workflows break down and precisely how to fix them.

My background spans 5+ years working within OCIO structures, managing institutional client relationships from $25M to $250M in AUM across endowments and foundations, contributing to over $2.5B in assets historically. That experience included navigating full GIPS compliance and verification — giving me a deep understanding of the operational and reporting complexity financial organizations face.

That foundation informs everything I build today. I help organizations automate reporting, deploy autonomous AI agents, and build the operational and business development infrastructure that scales — bringing a finance practitioner's perspective to every technical solution.
